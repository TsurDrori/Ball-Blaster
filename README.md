# 💣 Ball Blaster

A family arcade game built by me and my kids to pass time during the **Epic Fury war**.

When rockets and sirens became the background noise of daily life, we sat down together and built a game. This is it.

---

## What is it?

A browser-based arcade shooter inspired by the classic Ball Blast genre.

A cannon sits at the bottom of the screen and fires automatically. Numbered balls fall from the top — shoot them down before they reach you. Each hit reduces the ball's HP by 1. Destroy them all, survive the wave, collect coins, upgrade your cannon, and keep going.

## How to play

Open `game/index.html` in your browser — no server needed.

**Controls:**
- `←` `→` or `A` `D` — move the cannon
- The cannon fires automatically
- Buy upgrades between waves with coins collected from destroyed balls

## Upgrades

| Upgrade | Effect |
|---|---|
| ⚡ Fire Rate | Shoot faster |
| 💥 Power | More damage per bullet |
| 🎯 Multi-shot | Fire multiple bullets at once |
| 🔵 Ball Size | Bigger bullets, easier to hit |
| ❤️ Lives | Survive a ball reaching the bottom |

## Tech

Pure vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build step. Two `<canvas>` elements — one for the WebGL background, one for the game.
